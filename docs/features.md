# Features & Terminal Interactions

ChatForge is built for speed and power. Here are the core tools you have at your disposal.

## Terminal Inputs

- `Enter` - Sends your current prompt.
- `Shift+Enter` - Inserts a new line into your prompt instead of sending.
- `↑` / `↓` - Rapidly scroll backwards and forwards through your locally stored prompt history.
- `Ctrl+F` - Opens a sliding, glowing search bar that instantly highlights matches across the entire current chat.

## Sidebar Organization

The left-hand sidebar acts as your history catalog:

- **Searching**: Use the persistent search bar to quickly filter past session titles.
- **Pinning**: Hovering over a session and clicking the Pin (`📌`) icon will stick it to the absolute top of your chat list.
- **Renaming**: Click the Pencil icon on hover to open an inline editor and rename the session title.

## Message Interactions

Each message block in ChatForge is highly interactive, allowing you to seamlessly refine your prompts or control how the data is displayed.

![Message Block Features](file:///C:/Users/crafe/Desktop/pro/ChatForge/docs/images/chatforge_message_block_1774709657700.png)

### Refining Responses
- **Edit**: If the AI misunderstood your prompt, hit the `edit` button! It opens a large inline textarea where you can fix your prompt and "Save & Re-send".
- **Retry**: Not happy with a response? Hit `retry` (`⟳`) to seamlessly wipe the AI response and ask the exact same prompt again.

### Toggling Outputs
- **Starring**: Save critical responses by starring them (`⭐`). This adds a subtle glowing border to the message, making it easy to identify when scrolling quickly.
- **Raw Markdown View**: If ChatForge's built-in `react-markdown` renderer isn't formatting your data correctly, simply click the `eye` icon to view the raw markdown. 
- **Clipboard Output**: Instantly copy an AI's response to your clipboard using the `copy` icon.
